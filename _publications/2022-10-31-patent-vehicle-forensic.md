Jongmoo Choi, Samuel Woo, and **Inho Song**. Republic of Korea Patent **10-2022-0139234** — *granted*.

A method and computing device for vehicle forensic analysis: retrieving critical artifacts from modern in-vehicle storage (~1 GB/s generated data) and reconstructing deleted data through physical dump and metadata analysis. Developed in collaboration with the Korean National Police (SPO) under IITP and SPO funding.
